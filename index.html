Profile

Qingxi Peng, Ph.D, is currently the professor of the information engineering school at the Wuhan College. He is leader of the Hubei Provincial Universities Outstanding Young and Middle-aged Technological Innovation Team, Member of CCF,CAAI and IAENG(International Association of Engineers), the reviewer of SCI journals such as Knowledge-based Systems (CCF B), Future Generation Computer Systems (CCF C), Applied Intelligence (CCF C).

Dr. Peng got his Ph.D from computer school at Wuhan University in 2018,and Master's Degree at Hubei University. His research interests focus on machine learning, natural language processing and information retrieval. He hosted or participated in the research and development of several enterprise-level software systems.He had half a year of overseas work experience. In recent years, he has focused on the research and application of large-scale Web data acquisition and intelligent recommendation.
